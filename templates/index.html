LPU Careers

About LPU

LPU (Lovely Professional University) is famous for its massive campus, extensive infrastructure, and large student body, which includes international students. It's particularly known for its programs in Engineering and Business/MBA, offering industry-integrated courses and strong placement support from major companies. The university is also recognized for its focus on research, innovation, and fostering an entrepreneurial environment with unique facilities like high-tech labs and student-run ventures

Admission

Admission to Lovely Professional University (LPU) is primarily based on the LPU NEST (National Entrance and Scholarship Test) for most undergraduate programs. For postgraduate courses, admissions may be based on merit or specific entrance exams depending on the program. LPU also offers direct admission options for certain courses based on academic performance in previous qualifications. The university provides various scholarships and financial aid options to support students.

Courses

LPU offers a wide range of courses across various disciplines including Engineering, Management, Law, Design, Arts, Sciences, and more. The university provides undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. Notable courses include B.Tech, MBA, BBA, LLB, and various specialized programs in emerging fields. LPU emphasizes industry-relevant curricula, practical learning, and research opportunities to prepare students for the job market.

Placements

LPU has a strong placement record with numerous national and international companies recruiting from the campus. The university's dedicated placement cell works to connect students with potential employers through job fairs, campus interviews, and industry collaborations. LPU's focus on skill development, internships, and industry-oriented training helps enhance students' employability. Top recruiters include companies from IT, engineering, finance, and other sectors, offering competitive salary packages and diverse job roles.
